BAKERY DESCRIPTION

Welcome to Lisa's Sprinkled Cottage, where every bite is a delightful journey into the world of freshly baked goodness! Our bakery is a haven for pastry and cake lovers, offering an array of delectable treats crafted with love and the finest organic ingredients. From classic croissants and brownies to whimsical cupcakes and custom cakes, we pride ourselves on creating baked goods that not only taste amazing but also bring joy to your day. Whether you're stopping by for your morning brownie and a pastry or ordering a special cake for a celebration, LISAH’S SPRINKLED COTTAGE is here to make your moments sweeter. Come in and experience the warmth of our bakery, where you'll meet your love at first bite!
